Top Friction Points & Quick Wins

Highest-impact issues across all journeys, ranked by ease vs. impact.

⚠ Top Friction Points

  1. Email verification dead-end with no clear resend (J1)
  2. Co-parent invite buried in Settings (J3)
  3. Parent B must sign up before accepting invite (J3)
  4. Onboarding is a single long form with no progress indicator (J2)
  5. Hardcoded Polish button label on invite panel (J3)
  6. AI card creation gated behind paywall - free users never see the wow moment (J4)
  7. Custody "reference date" concept is confusing without explanation (J8)
  8. Upgrade wall is abrupt with no prior soft nudging (J10)
  9. Expense: creating a card and requesting payment are 2 separate steps (J6)
  10. No "Clear bought" undo in shopping list (J7)

✨ Quick Wins

  1. Add "Resend verification email" button + auto-advance when verified
  2. Surface "Invite co-parent" card on empty board after onboarding
  3. Fix hardcoded Polish on invite button
  4. Pre-fill Parent A name from Google OAuth during onboarding
  5. Add Yes/No quick-reply chips to attention strip cards (no dialog needed)
  6. Add Undo snackbar after "Clear bought items"
  7. Add tooltip for "reference date" in custody settings
  8. Make upgrade CTA context-specific (tied to blocked feature)
  9. Default upgrade CTA to "Start 14-day trial" not monthly price
  10. Show first sync confirmation toast after calendar connect
