Google Calendar integration

Do-Do may request access to your Google Calendar to sync family events, tasks, and reminders. This integration is entirely optional - you may use Do-Do without connecting Google Calendar.

  • Scope requested: https://www.googleapis.com/auth/calendar.events - allows Do-Do to read and write calendar events on your behalf.
  • What we read: event titles, dates, times, and descriptions for events created or modified in Do-Do.
  • What we write: Do-Do tasks, reminders, and agreed schedule items are created or updated as calendar events at your direction.
  • What we never do: Do-Do does not read, store, process, or share Google Calendar events that were not created by Do-Do. We do not scan your calendar for personal data, contacts, or any other purpose beyond family coordination.
  • Token storage: OAuth refresh tokens are stored encrypted in Supabase (EU, Ireland) and used solely to refresh calendar access. They are never shared with any third party.
  • Revoke access: at any time via myaccount.google.com/permissions or via Do-Do Settings - Calendar - Disconnect Google Calendar.
  • Google API Services User Data Policy: Do-Do's use and transfer of information received from Google APIs adheres to the Google API Services User Data Policy, including the Limited Use requirements. Information received from Google APIs is not used to develop, improve, or train generalized AI or ML models.

Sharing and processors

  • Supabase (EU-West-1, Ireland) - database, authentication, file storage, and real-time sync. Your data is stored in the EU.
  • Vercel (US) - serverless API hosting. Request data passes through Vercel's infrastructure.
  • Anthropic (US) - AI field extraction via Claude Haiku. Card text submitted for AI interpretation is sent to Anthropic's API. No data is used for model training without consent.
  • Stripe (US/EU) - payment processing for subscriptions and expense payments. Payment card data is handled exclusively by Stripe and never stored by Do-Do.
  • Resend (US) - transactional email delivery (reminders, invites, payment requests).
  • Google LLC (US) - Google Sign-In for authentication and optional Google Calendar sync. Data exchanged with Google is governed by Google's Privacy Policy and the Google API Services User Data Policy.
  • Data is shared with your invited co-parent according to family-board permissions.
  • Data may be shared with authorities when legally required.
